Brand Asset & Logo Authorization Agreement

E-Commerce Hosting & Promotion Permission
Date: June 30, 2026
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
1. Parties

This Brand Asset & Logo Authorization Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into and made effective as of the Effective Date, by and between:

  • The Developer: Rohith P, residing in Bengaluru, Karnataka (hereinafter referred to as the "Developer").
  • The Business: Shopwel Supermarket, located at Dream Paradise, 401/C, Gubbalala Main Road, Subramanyapura, Bengaluru 560061, represented by its authorized owner/manager (hereinafter referred to as the "Business").
2. Authorization & License Grant

The Business hereby grants the Developer a non-exclusive, royalty-free, license to host, display, publish, and use the trade name "Shopwel Supermarket", associated brand logos, store photographs, and product information (collectively, the "Brand Assets") on the e-commerce grocery store website deployed at https://shopwel.onrender.com/ (and any custom domain names configured for it, including https://shopwelsupermarket.in/).

This authorization is granted solely to allow the Developer to build, run, host, maintain, and generate promotional marketing materials (such as stickers, counter standees, and flyers containing the website QR code) for the Business.

3. Term & Lock-in Period

The parties agree to the following terms regarding the duration of this brand authorization:

  • Lock-in Period: This authorization is irrevocable and binding for an initial minimum term of six (6) months, commencing on July 1, 2026, and ending on December 31, 2026.
  • Rental Continuation: Following the expiration of the initial 6-month term, this brand authorization shall automatically continue to remain active as long as the Business continues to lease the website from the Developer and pays the agreed monthly website rental fees.
  • Termination: This license will immediately terminate upon the official termination of the website rental agreement. Upon termination, the Developer shall take the e-commerce website offline and remove the Business's Brand Assets from all servers.
4. Ownership Clarity

Nothing in this agreement shall be construed as a transfer of intellectual property. The Business retains all ownership rights, titles, and interests in its trademarks and logo assets. The Developer retains all ownership rights, titles, and interests in the website source code, systems, designs, and database architectures.

5. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by, and construed in accordance with, the laws of India. Any disputes arising under this agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Bengaluru, Karnataka.
