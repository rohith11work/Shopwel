Who We Are

Serving Subramanyapura Families Since Day One

Shopwel Supermarket has been the go-to destination for residents of Subramanyapura, Gubbalala, and the surrounding areas. Nestled in the Dream Paradise Building on Gubbalala Main Road, we bring you everything your family needs — fresh produce, dairy, branded snacks, household essentials, and even stationery — all under one roof.
